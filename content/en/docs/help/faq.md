## What is the theme used for this application?

The Recruitment Glossary uses a theme created by [h-enk](https://github.com/h-enk) called [doks](https://getdoks.org/).

## Keyboard shortcuts for search?

- focus: `Ctrl + /`
- select: `↓` and `↑`
- open: `Enter`
- close: `Esc`
